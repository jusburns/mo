About Mo

Mohammed (Mo) Abid is a User Experience and Interaction Designer currently pursuing his Masters of Human-Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon University. Previously, Mo was a user experience designer for Ramadan Kareem, a funded non-profit out of the Stanford d.school. He's also worked in graphic design at Tesla and finance at Apple after majoring in Economics at Stanford University.

You probably will never meet a bigger Stanford football, country music, or Golden State Warriors fan than Mo.
