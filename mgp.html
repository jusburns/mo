Ramadan Kareem

An app made by MGP, a funded non-profit out of the Stanford d.school. The app gives you a new charity to donate to for every day of Ramadan.

Role: User Experience Designer

Team: Professor Nadia Roumani, Kursat Ozenc, Margaret Hagan

The Problem

Due to the rampant corruption of governments in the Middle East, millenial Muslims are hesitant to donate to charities for fear of their money being diverted or spent primarily on overhead costs. Additionally, existing tools are onerous and don't provide info on new charities.

Needs-finding & Prototyping

We conducted contextual inquiries to identify the needs of our users. We took those needs and began visioning and testing prototypes.

The Solution

An Android and iPhone app that provides daily charitable suggestions based on a theme, such as “Widows,” for each of the 30 days of Ramadan. Each daily suggestion provides detailed statistics or an anecdote about the theme as well as options of organizations to donate to that help with that area.
